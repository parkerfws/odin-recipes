Spagetti Carbornara Recipe

Description

Spagetti Carbornara is a pasta dish made using an egg sauce and crispy pork either bacon or panchetta. A fair amount of parmasean and black pepper is used.

Ingridinets

  • 1lb Spagetti
  • 2 Egg Yolks
  • 1 Egg White
  • Parmasean Cheese
  • 1.5lb Bacon
  • Salt
  • Black Pepper
  • Heavy Cream

Instructions

  1. Slice the pack of bacon into small piecies
  2. Cook bacon in a skillit on low heat
  3. Boil water
  4. Add pasta to boiling water
  5. While pasta and bacon are cooking crack two whole eggs and 1 egg white into a small bowl
  6. Add half of the paramasean and add a heafty amount of black pepper to the same bowl as the eggs
  7. Whisk the bowl untill eggs are beaten and cheese and pepper are properly mixed
  8. When bacon is little over half way cooked add a 1/4 cup of heavy cream make sure to coat all the bacon this will help get the bacon extra crispy
  9. Save a cup of the pasta water
  10. When Pasta is aldente and bacon is finished cooking mix all Ingridinets in a bow and stir quickly
  11. If pasta gets dry add a little of the saved pasta water
  12. Salt to taste
