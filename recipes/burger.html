Burger

Description

A juicy beef burgur served on a toasted bun with lettuce,tomato and mayo.

Ingridinets

  • Ground beef
  • Garlic
  • Butter
  • Hamburger bun
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato

Instructions

  1. Roll ground beef into a large ball then flatten inot a hamburger patty
  2. Season patty with salt and pepper
  3. Heat a frying pan with olive oil
  4. Chop lettuce and tomato
  5. Place burger on the pan flipping it every 60 sec until medium rare
  6. After the burgur has been flipped once on each side add a tabel spoon of butter to the pan
  7. Use butter to baste until burger is fully cooked
  8. Assemble burger on top of the bun and topped with lettuce and tomato
